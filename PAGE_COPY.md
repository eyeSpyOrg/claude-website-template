## Beneficiaries ( /beneficiaries )

**H1:** Who the PAWty Helps

**Intro:** Every wagging tail on the orange carpet supports two organizations
working to help people navigate the world with confidence.

### Eye Spy Foundation
At Eye Spy, we help the blind and low vision community navigate community
resources so they can live a better low vision lifestyle. We greet everyone with a
hug or a handshake — because connection comes first — and we spend our days
pointing people toward the programs, services, and tools that make everyday life
more accessible. The Fashion PAWty is how we bring the whole community together to
celebrate that mission.

### Leader Dogs for the Blind
[ORIGINAL DRAFT — send to Leader Dogs for approval]
Leader Dogs for the Blind is a guide dog school that raises and trains dogs to help
people who are blind or low vision travel safely and independently. From playful
puppies to confident working guides, each dog is prepared to become a trusted
navigation partner — opening up new freedom for the person at the other end of the
harness. This year, the Eye Spy Foundation is proud to sponsor one Leader Dogs
puppy, and a portion of every dollar raised at The Fashion PAWty goes directly to
their work. When you party with us, you help raise a future guide dog.

## FAQ ( /faq )

**H1:** Frequently Asked Questions

1. **What is The Fashion PAWty?**
   It's a doggie fashion show and community celebration — "A Navigation
   Celebration" — hosted by the Eye Spy Foundation. Dogs strut the orange carpet
   while the community enjoys mocktails, food, games, and music, all to support the
   blind and low vision community and Leader Dogs for the Blind.

2. **When and where is it?**
   Saturday, September 26, 2026, from 4 to 7 PM at Happy Brew, 3200 Hendricks Ave,
   Jacksonville, FL 32207.

3. **How much are tickets and what's included?**
   Tickets are $30 and include entry to the show, food, and mocktails.

4. **Who does the event benefit?**
   The Eye Spy Foundation and Leader Dogs for the Blind. Eye Spy is also sponsoring
   one Leader Dogs puppy this year.

5. **Can I bring my dog?**
   This year's fashion show features a curated lineup of dogs, so you don't need to
   bring your own — come enjoy the show, the mocktails, and the fun. It's a
   people-first celebration.

6. **How do the dogs get in the fashion show?**
   The runway lineup is organized by our team for this year's event. Want to be part
   of a future PAWty? Email team@eyespy.org and say hello.

7. **Is the event accessible?**
   Yes — the entire venue is accessible, and accessibility is at the heart of
   everything we do. Park in the church lot next door on Hendricks Avenue (overflow
   behind the church). Questions about your visit? Email team@eyespy.org and we'll help.

8. **How can I sponsor or get involved?**
   Head to our Sponsors page to claim a named party area, or email team@eyespy.org.
   You can also grab merch at shop.thefashionpawty.com — every bit supports the cause.
